Boating in Kerala

Boating in Kerala is a captivating experience that allows you to explore the serene backwaters and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this enchanting state. Let me share some insights about boating in Kerala: Houseboats (Kettuvallams): Houseboats, locally known as Kettuvallams, are the iconic vessels used for boating in Kerala. These traditional boats are made by tying hundreds of jackwood planks together using coir knots. The top layer is coated with a caustic black resin extracted from boiled cashew nut kernels.
