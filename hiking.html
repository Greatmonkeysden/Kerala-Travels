Hiking in Kerala

Hiking in Kerala offers a unique opportunity to explore the lush greenery and stunning landscapes of the Western Ghats. With numerous trails ranging from easy to challenging, hikers can experience the beauty of Kerala's forests, waterfalls, and wildlife.

Popular hiking destinations in Kerala include Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady. These areas offer picturesque views, diverse flora and fauna, and opportunities to interact with local communities.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hiker, Kerala's trails have something to offer for everyone. So put on your hiking boots, pack your backpack, and embark on an adventure amidst nature's bounty!
